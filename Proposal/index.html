03 — Tournament Structure

Two Brackets,
One Grand Final

Solo runs a deeper field of 32; Duo pairs run Scotch Doubles from a field of 16 teams.

Solo (Singles) — 32 Players
Duo (Scotch Doubles) — 16 Teams
Re-Entry Rules

One official entry per player/team. Re-entry is permitted only during Round 1 (Round of 32 for Solo / Round of 16 for Duo). Once Round 1 concludes, registrations and re-entries are closed.
